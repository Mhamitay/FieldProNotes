RiseFSM System Architecture Summary
===================================

SYSTEM TYPE: Field Service Management for Oil & Gas Industry
TECHNOLOGY: .NET 6 Razor Pages, Vue.js 2, SQL Server
ARCHITECTURE: Multi-tenant, Offline-first, Domain-driven

CORE BUSINESS ENTITIES:
======================

1. Cost Items (tcCostItem) - Master service catalog
2. Price Items (tcPriceItem) - Customer-specific pricing
3. Jobs (tJob) - Central work orders
4. Tickets (tcTicket) - Billable line items
5. Load Out (tLoadOut) - Equipment deployment tracking
6. Quotes (tcQuote) - Customer proposals

TECHNICAL STACK:
================

Backend: .NET 6, Entity Framework Core, SQL Server
Frontend: Vue.js 2, TypeScript, Bootstrap 4
Data: Multi-tenant with TenantID isolation
Offline: IndexedDB with background sync

BUSINESS WORKFLOWS:
==================

Sales: Inquiry ? Quote ? Negotiation ? Award
Operations: Planning ? Deployment ? Work ? Documentation
Financial: Tracking ? Billing ? Invoice ? Payment

KEY FEATURES:
=============

- Multi-tenant data isolation
- Offline-first operation
- Equipment-to-billing integration
- Approval workflows for cost control
- Real-time business intelligence
